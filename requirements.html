Recommended System Requirements

The Workstation can be used in different ways, depending on the scientific use case. For example, basic image browsing can be done on a laptop, while heavy 3d tracing requires a more powerful desktop workstation. Here we list two minimal system configurations.

As an alternative to purchasing a high-end machine, users can leverage a virtualized environment described below.

Fly Image Browsing

  • 2.9 GHz Intel Core i5 CPU or better
  • 8 GB RAM (16 GB recommended)
  • 2 GB of Video RAM
  • 100 GB of available hard-disk space (1 TB recommended)
  • Mac OS X 10.11, Windows 7, or Scientific Linux 7

Mouse Neuron Tracing

  • 2.6 GHz Intel Core i7 CPU or better
  • 48 GB of RAM (64 GB recommended)
  • 4 GB of Video RAM (8 GB recommended)
  • 1 GB of disk space
  • Windows 7 or Scientific Linux 7
